Module Specifications

Six modules, deeply specified

Each module is scoped below with features, data requirements, and integration points.

📦
Module 01

Inventory Management

MVP Core

The foundation module. Every sale logged here automatically updates stock and feeds bookkeeping. Offline-first — works without internet using local SQLite, syncing to Supabase on reconnect.

📋 Product Catalog

Add products with name, SKU, category, cost price, selling price, and reorder threshold. Supports barcode/QR scanning via phone camera.

🔢 Stock Tracking

Real-time quantity tracking. Every sale deducts automatically. Manual adjustments for returns, spoilage, and stock takes.

⚠️ Low-Stock Alerts

Push notifications and SMS when stock falls below reorder threshold. Configurable per product.

🏭 Supplier Management

Link products to suppliers. Track lead times and contact details for fast restocking.

📊 Stock Valuation

Total inventory value at cost and at selling price. Understocked vs. overstocked views.

📶 Offline Mode

All operations queue locally. Syncs automatically when connectivity is restored. No data loss.

Integration hooks: Every sale → deducts stock quantity + creates a bookkeeping income entry + logs against customer profile (if known).
📒
Module 02

Bookkeeping & Payments

MVP Core

Automated financial records driven by real transactions. No manual data entry. Integrates with mobile money and bank APIs to capture payments automatically.

💰 Income Tracking

Every logged sale creates an income entry. Manual income entry for non-sale revenue (e.g. services, consulting).

💸 Expense Tracking

Log expenses by category (stock purchase, rent, transport, salaries). Attach photo receipts via phone camera.

📄 Invoicing

Generate and send professional PDF invoices by WhatsApp or email. Track payment status (sent, viewed, paid).

📱 Mobile Money

Flutterwave/Paystack integration for M-Pesa, Airtel Money, MTN MoMo. Payments auto-reconcile to bookkeeping.

📈 P&L Reports

Automatic weekly, monthly, and annual Profit & Loss statements. Exportable as PDF for bank or investor submissions.

🧾 Tax Summaries

VAT tracking and simple tax summaries by period. Configurable by country tax rules.

Key differentiator: The bookkeeping data is the engine for the Funding Match module. Verified transaction history = investor-ready financial profile, automatically.
👥
Module 03

Customer Relationship Manager

Phase 3

Turns every sale into a customer record. SMEs know who buys what, when they last came back, and how to reach them — without any manual data entry.

📇 Customer Profiles

Name, phone, location, tags (VIP, wholesale, new). Auto-created on first purchase.

🕐 Purchase History

Full timeline of every transaction per customer. Average spend, visit frequency, favourite products.

📲 SMS Follow-ups

Automated messages via Africa's Talking: "Hi Grace, we restocked your favourite Bliss body lotion 🧴" — personalised, affordable.

🏆 Loyalty Tracking

Simple points or stamp-card system. Reward loyal customers without complicated apps.

🎯 Segmentation

Filter by spend, frequency, last seen, or product preference. Target campaigns to the right people.

📣 Bulk Campaigns

Send SMS to a segment: "10% off this Saturday for our top 20 customers." Track opens and redemptions.

Module 04

Business Analytics

Phase 3

Not charts. Answers. The AI layer reads transaction data and produces plain-language weekly summaries any business owner can act on — regardless of education level.

📅 Daily Dashboard

Today's sales, expenses, net profit, and top-selling product — at a glance on the app home screen.

🤖 AI Weekly Digest

"Your best day this week was Thursday. Bliss lotion outsold everything 3:1. Consider doubling your order." — powered by Claude API.

📉 Trend Detection

Spots rising and falling products before the owner notices. Flags seasonality patterns from historical data.

⏰ Peak Hours

When does the shop get busiest? When should staff be present? Shown visually and in plain language.

💹 Margin Analysis

Which products make the most profit? Which sell well but barely break even? Ranked by profitability.

🔮 Smart Forecasting

Simple demand forecasting: "Based on your last 3 months, you'll need ~40 units of rice next week."

-- Key database trigger: auto-update inventory on sale
CREATE OR REPLACE FUNCTION deduct_stock_on_sale()
RETURNS TRIGGER AS $$
BEGIN
  UPDATE products
  SET quantity_in_stock = quantity_in_stock - NEW.quantity
  WHERE id = NEW.product_id
    AND business_id = (
      SELECT business_id FROM sales WHERE id = NEW.sale_id
    );
  RETURN NEW;
END;
$$ LANGUAGE plpgsql;

CREATE TRIGGER trigger_deduct_stock
AFTER INSERT ON sale_items
FOR EACH ROW EXECUTE FUNCTION deduct_stock_on_sale();

-- Key view: monthly P&L per business
CREATE VIEW monthly_pnl AS
SELECT
  s.business_id,
  date_trunc('month', s.sold_at) AS month,
  SUM(si.quantity * si.unit_price) AS revenue,
  SUM(si.quantity * si.cost_price_snapshot) AS cogs,
  SUM(si.quantity * si.unit_price) - SUM(si.quantity * si.cost_price_snapshot) AS gross_profit
FROM sales s
JOIN sale_items si ON si.sale_id = s.id
WHERE s.status = 'completed'
GROUP BY s.business_id, date_trunc('month', s.sold_at);
API Design

RESTful API Endpoints

Built on Express.js + Supabase. All endpoints require a Bearer JWT (Supabase Auth). Row-level security ensures businesses can only access their own data.

Inventory

GET/api/products
List all active products for the authenticated business. Query params: ?category= ?low_stock=true ?search=
POST/api/products
Create a product. Body: name sku cost_price selling_price quantity_in_stock reorder_threshold
PUT/api/products/:id/adjust-stock
Manual stock adjustment. Body: adjustment (positive or negative) reason (return | spoilage | stocktake | purchase)

Sales & Bookkeeping

POST/api/sales
Record a sale. Body: customer_id (optional) payment_method items: [{product_id, quantity, unit_price}]. Auto-triggers stock deduction.
GET/api/reports/pnl
Profit & Loss for a period. Query params: ?from= ?to= ?period=weekly|monthly|annual
POST/api/expenses
Log an expense. Body: category amount description receipt_url (Supabase Storage URL)

CRM

GET/api/customers
List customers. Query params: ?tag=vip ?inactive_days=30 (lapsed customers) ?search=
POST/api/campaigns/sms
Send SMS campaign. Body: customer_ids: [] or segment: {tag, min_spend} message. Routes via Africa's Talking.

Analytics & AI

GET/api/analytics/dashboard
Today's KPIs: sales total, expenses, profit, top product, units sold. Cached every 5 minutes.
GET/api/analytics/ai-digest
Returns cached weekly AI insight. Regenerated every Sunday via cron job. Calls Claude API with 7-day transaction summary.

Funding

GET/api/funding/matches
Returns list of funding opportunities the business is eligible for, ranked by match score. Calculated from revenue, age, sector, and women_led fields.
GET/api/funding/profile
Returns the business's investor-ready financial profile: 6-month revenue, growth rate, margin, and sector. Shareable via unique URL.

Competition Strategy

Pitch notes by judge type

Different judges score on different criteria. Here's how to frame SME360 for each one.

🏛️ Impact/SDG Judge

Lead with: women-led business visibility + gender-lens funding match. Tie to SDG 5 (gender equality), SDG 8 (decent work), SDG 10 (reduced inequalities). Quantify: "Every SME on SME360 becomes visible to impact investors for the first time."

💻 Tech/Product Judge

Lead with: offline-first architecture + Claude API integration. Show the database trigger that keeps inventory and bookkeeping in sync. Demonstrate the 30-second sale flow live. Emphasise: one codebase, two platforms.

💼 Business Judge

Lead with: $5/month price point + three revenue streams. Show the unit economics: at 1,000 paying users → $5,000 MRR before transaction fees. The B2B investor data product is the long-term play — mention it but don't over-explain.

🌍 Market Judge

Lead with: offline-first + Africa's Talking SMS + mobile money. Show that you understand connectivity realities. Name the specific markets you'd pilot first and why. Having even one real pilot user beats the most polished prototype.

The one thing that wins competitions

Walk in with a real business owner who used the app this week. Not a demo. Not a prototype. An actual person who logged sales, checked their stock, and read an AI insight — and can say what it changed for their business. That one user is worth more than 100 polished slides. Start building, start piloting, and let the real story sell itself.
